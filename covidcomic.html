Ways to Protect Yourself & Others from COVID-19 with Bobi the Fluffian

Wear a mask when you are out in public spaces

Do's
  • Clean your hands before putting on or removing the mask
  • Inspect the mask for damage or if dirty
  • Adjust the mask so it covers your mouth, nose and chin fully
  • Avoid touching the mask
  • Remove the mask by the straps without touching your face
  • Store the mask in a clean ziploc bag and wash it often in hot, soapy water
Don'ts
  • Don't use a soiled, wet or loose mask!
  • Don't wear the mask without covering your nose
  • Don't share your mask with others
  • Don't remove the mask within 1 metre of other people
  • Don't wear a mask that's hard to breath in

Stay at least 2 meters (6 feet) from others

When someone coughs, sneezes, or speaks they spray small liquid droplets from their nose or mouth which may contain the virus, which you may breath in and catch

Avoid touching your face, especially your eyes, nose, and mouth

Your hands may be dirty and covered with the virus

Keep yours hands clean! Wash your hands with soap and water for at least 20s

Washing your hands with soap and water or using alcohol-based hand rub kills viruses that may be on your hands

Cough or sneeze into your elbow, not your hands (or someone else)

The virus can be carried by liquid droplets from your sneeze and be passed on to someone else
